## Move-in setup checklist: 10 tasks for a new home

**TV mounting and media setup**

1. Mount the primary living room TV on the wall — choose the height and viewing angle before the furniture arrives.
2. Mount a bedroom TV if needed — this is easiest before dressers and nightstands are in place.
3. Route and conceal surface cables where the wall cannot be fished — a clean cable channel keeps the setup looking intentional.
4. Install a soundbar mount or position a small soundbar shelf below the TV.

**Furniture assembly**

5. Assemble bed frames, headboards, and platform beds — these take longer than the box suggests and benefit from two sets of hands.
6. Assemble office desks, bookshelves, and storage units before the room fills in around them.
7. Assemble outdoor furniture — Bressi Ranch and Oceanside homeowners often move in during spring or summer when patio setup is an immediate priority.

**Wall anchoring and installations**

8. Anchor tall furniture to the wall — bookshelves, dressers, and wardrobe units should be secured especially in California seismic zones.
9. Install floating shelves, gallery wall layouts, or wall-mounted storage in the locations you marked during planning.
10. Hang mirrors, large artwork, and any heavy wall décor — proper anchoring matters more than most homeowners realize until something falls.

For a new Bressi Ranch or Oceanside home, most of the TV and media setup plus two or three furniture assembly tasks fit into a single focused visit. A full move-in setup covering all ten checklist items typically runs two visits or one longer day depending on room count and furniture volume.
