## Turnover punch list: 12 repairs before move-in

**Doors and hardware**

1. Adjust any interior doors that stick, rub, or do not latch properly.
2. Tighten or replace door handles, knobs, and strike plates showing wear.
3. Replace missing or damaged door stops behind every interior door.
4. Check closet door tracks and rollers — these are frequently damaged during moves.

**Walls and ceilings**

5. Patch anchor holes, picture nail holes, and any small gouges from furniture moves.
6. Inspect corners and stairwells for drywall dings or corner bead damage.
7. Check for any texture mismatches from previous patch repairs — these show badly in showing photos.

For larger patch work or texture matching, see [drywall repair in Vista](/service-areas/vista/drywall-repair/) or [drywall repair in San Marcos](/service-areas/san-marcos/drywall-repair/).

**Bathroom fixtures**

8. Tighten or replace towel bars, robe hooks, and toilet paper holders.
9. Replace toilet seats that are cracked, stained, or wobbly.
10. Check under-sink areas for slow drips at supply lines or P-traps.

**Kitchen and utility**

11. Test garbage disposal and tighten any loose faucet handles or aerators.
12. Replace cracked outlet covers, missing vent covers, and any damaged switch plates throughout the unit.
