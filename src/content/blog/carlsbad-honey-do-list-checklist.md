## Carlsbad honey-do checklist (20 quick wins)

**Entry and living areas**

1. Tighten loose door handles and strike plates.
2. Adjust sticky interior doors that rub.
3. Replace worn door stops and bumpers.
4. Patch small wall dings and nail holes.
5. Re-caulk baseboards where gaps have opened.

**Kitchen** 6. Replace cabinet hardware that pulls loose. 7. Re-level cabinet doors that sag. 8. Tighten faucet handles and aerators. 9. Replace worn caulk at backsplash edges. 10. Secure loose drawer slides.

**Bathrooms** 11. Replace toilet seats that wobble. 12. Tighten towel bars and robe hooks. 13. Touch up grout or caulk around the tub. 14. Fix door latches that do not catch. 15. Replace worn exhaust fan covers.

**Bedrooms and hallways** 16. Adjust closet doors and tracks. 17. Secure loose shelf brackets. 18. Patch small drywall cracks at corners.

**Exterior quick fixes** 19. Repair a loose gate latch or fence hardware. 20. Replace worn weatherstripping at the front door.

## Local tips for Carlsbad homes

- Coastal air can loosen exterior hardware faster than you expect. Prioritize gates, hinges, and latches.
- Builder-grade hardware is often the first place small upgrades feel noticeable.
- Touch-ups before guests or seasonal rentals deliver the biggest return in comfort and appearance.
