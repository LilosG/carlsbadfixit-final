## What You Should Avoid: DIY, Unlicensed Contractors, and Overpriced Shops

### DIY Temptation

A YouTube video makes drywall repair or electrical outlet installation look easy. Sometimes it is. But in Encinitas, where homes are valuable and coastal conditions are harsh, a mistake gets expensive fast. A drywall patch that wasn't primed properly will bubble and crack. An electrical outlet installed incorrectly creates a fire hazard. A TV mount that misses the stud will rip out of your wall and damage your TV.

The time you save doing it yourself often costs you double when you have to pay someone to fix it.

### Unlicensed Contractors

You'll see flyers, Craigslist posts, and Facebook ads from contractors without licenses or insurance. Their rates seem low, and they might do decent work on one job. But if something goes wrong—damage to your home, injury on your property, a repair that fails—you have no recourse. You're also supporting someone who's not following local codes or paying taxes.

Encinitas has strong building standards and code enforcement. A licensed professional knows how to work within those rules and gets permits when they're needed.
